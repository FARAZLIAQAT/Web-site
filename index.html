Welcome

Welcome to Word International Ministries, where our mission is to win souls for Jesus Christ and make disciples. We are a Pentecostal ministry trusting in the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

About Us

Our founder, Rev. Pastor Naveed Khursheed, was born into a pastor's family and is a third-generation servant of God. He has dedicated his life to spreading the Gospel and meeting the needs of people.

Our Ministries

  • Children Ministry: Providing education and support to underprivileged children.
  • Helping Poor: Reaching out to those in need with financial support and resources.
  • Women's Ministry: Empowering women through spiritual growth and development.
  • Village Ministry: Reaching rural areas with the Gospel and providing support.
  • Outreach Ministry: Spreading the Gospel through conventions, crusades, and pastoral networks.

Our Mission

Our purpose is to build the Kingdom of God by meeting the needs of people. We believe in:

  • Salvation: (1 Tim 2:3-4)
  • Financial Support: (Ecc 4:9-12)
  • Spirituality: (Acts 17:26-27)
  • Succession: (Ecc 2:24)
